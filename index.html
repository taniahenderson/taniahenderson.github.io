Who We Are

Welcome to Pando Systems, where innovation meets collaboration. At the heart of our mission is the commitment to empower educators and students alike through our cutting-edge web application, which has been crafted to show that teaching and student engagement aren't limited to the confines of a traditional classroom.

We strive to redefine the educational experience by providing a seamless and interactive online environment that promotes creativity, communication, and cooperative learning. At Pando Systems, we envisioned a digital collaboration platform that could host a diverse community where ideas can flourish and be freely exchanged.


Our Products

Pando Sketch

Featuring an intuitive interface, Pando Sketch enables its users to access a dynamic visual workspace seamlessly integrated with real-time chat functionality. It’s more than just a tool; it’s a gateway to a world where educators and learners can organise, create, share, and collaborate effortlessly.

  • Learning

    • Work on group projects, brainstorm big ideas, seek help on challenging topics - our platform encourages peer-to-peer interaction and teamwork

  • Built for teams

    • Invite your students to your online classroom, or get together with your friends to create a fun workspace

  • Use on any device

    • Available to use on any common web browser and on mobile/tablet devices

  • Teaching

    • Organised, flexible workspace to fashion lessons, demonstrate complex concepts, and interact with students in real time

  • Super simple

    • Utilises an intuitive and accessible interface, ensuring that users of all technical abilities can engage and contribute

  • Security

    • Through the use of admin controls, you can control who your workspace is available to - restrict access to your classroom, share only with selected peers, or display your Project on our Explore page to connect with the global community
